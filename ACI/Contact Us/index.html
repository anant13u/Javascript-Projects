Introduction

The Association of Computer Institutes (ACI) is a prominent national-level organization registered under the Society Registration Act XXI of 1860, bearing Registration No-66879, New Delhi. Founded on September 9, 2009, ACI operates as a not-for-profit entity with a mission to empower youth and stimulate entrepreneurship across India. By focusing on vocational training and skill development, ACI is instrumental in shaping the future of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and ensuring that the Indian workforce is equipped to thrive in a rapidly evolving economy.
