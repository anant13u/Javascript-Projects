The Association of Computer Institutes (ACI) is a prominent national-level organization registered under the Society Registration Act XXI of 1860, bearing Registration No-66879, New Delhi. Founded on September 9, 2009, ACI operates as a not-for-profit entity with a mission to empower youth and stimulate entrepreneurship across India. By focusing on vocational training and skill development, ACI is instrumental in shaping the future of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and ensuring that the Indian workforce is equipped to thrive in a rapidly evolving economy.

Vision

To be a catalyst for economic growth by empowering individuals through skill development and entrepreneurship, ensuring sustainable livelihoods, and fostering innovation across all sectors.

About ACI

Established in 2009, ACI is a leading not-for-profit organization dedicated to entrepreneurship development and youth empowerment in India. ACI works closely with national and international bodies, including the Directorate General of Employment & Training (DGET) and the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, playing a key role in policy formulation for MSME growth. Since 2015, ACI has served as a skill assessment body, evaluating the competencies of over 40,000 candidates with the expertise of more than 350 highly qualified assessors under the DGET-Modular Employable Skills (MES) Courses.
